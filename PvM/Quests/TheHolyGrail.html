THIS IS IMPORTANT STUFF

  • The quest starts in the Throne Room in Lord British Castle. There are some VERY important things to remember:
  • This is a Quest Chain. Be sure to Accept and Continue when completing each part of the quest to advance to the next Scene.
  • If you close the quest dialog instead of the above, you will miss out on the items they are giving you and you will have to do that part of the quest again.
  • Be sure to read what items you need to obtain and mark as Quest Items in each Scene.
  • When you come across a side quest in your travels, be sure to do that quest also. They are going to give you an item you need to complete the Scene.
  • You are only able to interact with the Grail Questers when you are on that part of the quest. This includes all interactions: accepting, killing, etc.
  • There are escorts in this quest, so get with a partner who can gate and do the Quest together! The spawn rates are set appropriately.
  • Have fun. The dialog is from the actual movie script.

MORE IMPORTANT NFORMATION

  • If there is a locked gate, then one of the NPC's you kill will have a key. Search their corpses carefully.
  • Once you are through the gate it will relock.
