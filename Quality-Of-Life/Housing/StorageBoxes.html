Storage Boxes

Located at the Magincia Stone Exchange!
 

The recent addition of the storage boxes to UOAlive is an amazing improvement for Quality of Life.

Those that regularly do crafting and imbuing will find these particularly useful.

There are a range of different boxes available to store categories of items, some are sorted by resource type, others by expansions.

All Boxes can be retrieved from Locked Down location in your house, moved to another house, re-secured and will still contain all contents!

All boxes can be dyed with a Furniture Dye Tub.

Storage Boxes must be Locked Down in a house to work. This is to avoid infinite inventory exploits.

The general Gump for most boxes will be blank until at least one of that type of item is added by either:
- Dropping the item directly onto the container if you are near it or,
- Using the "Add Resource" button at the top left then targeting the item in your pack. This can be done from a large distance from the chest.

This is what makes them particularly useful for crafters since you can open a number of boxes, move around your house, and still access all of your materials.
No more "I ran out of X" or "I forgot to grab X" moments!
It's a simple button press to remove some rather than running back to your storage.
