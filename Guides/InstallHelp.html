Importing Classic UO Profiles

To import your profiles it's as simple as copy and paste.
Go into your existing Classic UO Folder and copy your Profiles.
(Something like the path below, to wherever you have it installed)
C:\Users\USER NAME\FILE LOCATION\Ultima Online Files\ClassicUOLauncher\ClassicUO\Data
There you will find 3 Folders.
These 3 folders contain
Screen Size, Hot Keys, Macros, Container Settings, Etc.
 

The Profiles Folder can be copied and pasted into your NEW Classic UO Data Folder.
The NEW Classic UO Data Folder will look like the image below.
 

Paste your old Profiles folder into the new Data Folder.
It will look like this now.
If a Profiles Folder already exists, you can paste and overwrite it.
 

Now your Character Settings are copied!

If you don't have any Razor Enhanced Profiles to be transferred hit
LAUNCH
to enjoy UOAlive
 

Importing Razor Enhanced Profiles

Now we will copy and paste your Razor Enhanced settings.
Open up your old Classic UO folder. You will see the Plugins Folder.
Open it.

There will be a Razor Enhanced folder.
Open it.
 

We want to copy your old Profiles Folder and your old Scripts Folder.
 

Now back inside your UOAlive Folder go into the Plugins Folder.

There you will find a Razor Enhanced folder.

Open the folder Razor Enhance.
Paste your old Profiles Folder and your old Scripts Folder.
You will be prompted with a Replace or Skip Files window.
Click 

Now your Razor Enhanced Settings are copied!
