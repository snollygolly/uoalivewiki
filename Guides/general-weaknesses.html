Undead- Fire, unless fighting a hellsteed which is unlikely 

Reptile- Cold or poison, some are weak to one but strong against the other 

Demon- Cold or energy with a few exceptions 

Repond- Fire or physical with a few exceptions 

Elemental- No prefered element, AI or consecrate weapon should be used 

Fey- Fire with a few exceptions 

Arachnid- Fire 

Each slayer group has their exceptions. For example a white wyrm is not weak to cold like most reptiles. It's weakest is fire which is different from most other reptiles. If you are unsure of what is the weakest for a specific enemy, it is best to look into it on UOGuide for the specific resistances. This should help with general use for those who would like a quick reference.
