The Murder Bear

A Legendary varient of the Rideable Polar Bear. This is a special bear with a low percent chance to spawn. It can be found in the Northern most tips of Tokuno. It is a dangerous area but worth the rewards of taming one of these Legendary creatures!

Stats: TBD

Special Abilities: Bushido

Spawn Method: A random chance to spawn from the normal Polar Bears in the Northern tip of Tokuno.
