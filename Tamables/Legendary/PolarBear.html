The Legendary Polar Bear

A Legendary variant of the Rideable Polar Bear. This is a special bear with a low percent chance to spawn. It can be found in the Northern most tips of Tokuno. It is a dangerous area, but worth the rewards of taming one of these Legendary creatures!
