There are 2 quest NPC's that offer a set of initiation armor. They can be found in the Warrior's Area of New Haven, just south of your spawn and the bank!

  • Captain Dasha for a human set (make sure to pick properly!)
  • Captain Luek for a gargoyle set. (make sure to pick properly!)

All you need is to use your Young Player Ticket, which they will ask for, to turn in for a set of armor.

You need to accept the quest and mark your new player ticket as a quest item(left click self toggle quest item) and turn it in to get the armor.

You can find this in your bank when creating a new character!

Enjoy!
