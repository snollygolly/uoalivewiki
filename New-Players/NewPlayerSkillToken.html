Did you know as a new player you can get ONE New Player Skill token? To encourage people to join our forums and mailing list for event updates and such(you can unsubscribe at anytime) we are giving away a 5 X 90 skill token to anyone who

1: Sign up on the forums.
2: Join our discord.

From there you go to the help ticket channel. Click the icon there to open a ticket. Then once the ticket has opened, first introduce yourself, we are not robots and appreciate humanity, then yell us your forum name and in game character name/account letting us know you are here for your Token!

We will need a few things as well in that ticket:

1. Discord Name:
2. Account Name:
3. Character Name:
4. Forum Name:
5. Rules Read & Acknowledged:
6: How you found UOAlive and maybe something else nice to the New Player Companions! They are human too and enjoy a good intro!

Also helpful if you willing is your time zone and typical play time or availability. This will help the NPCs ( @New Player Companion ) to make sure one of us can meet up with you in a timely fashion. After submitting this information a @New Player Companion will setup a time to meet you in game to give you your token! You must be at the keyboard and present to receive your token. Make sure to have time to set aside so we can get you all squared away! 

Limited one per person! Must be a NEW Player. You can use it on any character, note, it cannot be used on a character with over 200 skills.

Get them while they last and welcome to UOAlive!
